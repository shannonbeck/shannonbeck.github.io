Preparing for the SANS GIAC Reverse Engineering in Malware (GREM) Exam

In 2024, I took the 6-day SANS For610 "Reverse-Engineering Malware: Malware Analysis Tools and Techniques" course. This intensive course was really an opening to understanding Windows OS internals and fundamentals needed for reverse engineering (RE). I successfully passed the exam in early 2025.

In preparing for the GREM exam, I spent many hours re-reading and re-watching videos. I also used the following resources in my prepation of my exam index (all 543 entries), and I am passing them along in case they can help you in your RE journey.

Many thanks to Lenny Zeltser for many of the resources listed below, as well course content in addition to Anuj Soni.
